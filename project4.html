Seven

★ 8.6/10

Mystery, Drama, Crime

Detective William Somerset is a veteran criminal detective who dreams of retiring and moving away from the city and the sinful inhabitants. 7 days before retirement, two troubles fall on Somerset: a young partner, Mills, and a particularly sophisticated murder. The sharp mind of an experienced detective immediately determines that this crime is likely to be followed by others. The news confirms his hunch. Realizing that the killer is punishing his victims for their mortal sins, the detective faces a choice: return to work or leave and turn the case over to his less experienced partner?
  • Director:David Fincher
  • Writer:Andrew Kevin Walker
  • Stars:Morgan Freeman • Brad Pitt • Kevin Spacey
  • Release Date: 1995
  • Runtime: 127 min
