Garfield

★ 6.8/10

Comedy, Fantasy

The owner of Garfield's cat, John, brings home a cute puppy, Odie, and from that moment Garfield's life goes awry. He wants only one thing — for the dog to disappear forever from his life and from his home. But when suddenly a puppy is abducted, Garfield, perhaps for the first time in his life, feels the responsibility that lies on him. And begins to act.
